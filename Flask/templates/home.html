Who We Aim to Serve


Our website is dedicated to supporting cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy and experiencing hair loss. We aim to provide personalized insights and resources to help individuals navigate this challenging journey.


Understanding Chemotherapy


Chemotherapy is a common cancer treatment that uses powerful drugs to target and destroy cancer cells in the body. While effective, it often leads to various side effects, including hair loss. We are here to help cancer patients understand and cope with this aspect of their treatment.


Predictive Hair Loss Model


Our website utilizes a machine learning model specifically trained to predict the likelihood and extent of hair loss during chemotherapy. By inputting relevant information, such as treatment type and duration, our model can provide patients with valuable insights to better prepare them for the potential effects on their appearance.




Improving Mental State


We understand that hair loss can have a significant impact on an individual's mental and emotional well-being. Alongside our predictive model, we will offer a range of resources, guidance, and support to help patients maintain a positive mindset, boost self-confidence, and enhance their overall mental state throughout their cancer journey in the near future.




Community and Support


Connecting with others who have faced similar challenges can be empowering and reassuring. Our website will provide a community forum where cancer patients, survivors, and their loved ones can come together to share experiences, exchange advice, and find solace in a supportive environment in the near future.
